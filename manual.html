Introduction

Thank you for your interest in TANGUY Web Audio Synthesizer.

System Requirements

TANGUY sounds best in Google Chrome, Opera, or Apple Safari.

Controls

TANGUY is a monosynth: only one note can be played from the keyboard at a time. Click the keys or play TANGUY using the home row on your QWERTY keyboard. The home row represents the white keys. Trigger the black keys from the row above (w = C#, e = D#, etc.).

Quickly shift the range of the keyboard up or down using the buttons -1 and +1 buttons or their corresponding keyboard shortcuts: - and = .

Choosing a Preset

Click on the program and choose a preset from the list.

Delay

Reverb effects can be achieved by setting a very short delay with an amount at roughly 50%.

An overdrive (not really overdrive) effect can be achieved by setting delay to the lowest possible setting and amount above 50% (probably happens at a lower percentage -- test).
